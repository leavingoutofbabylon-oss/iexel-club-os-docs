# IEXEL Club OS MVP Status

> **Overall estimate:** approximately 83% complete
> **Updated:** 14 July 2026

The estimate reflects implemented, usable capability rather than the number of ideas on the long-term roadmap. Football operations are substantially complete through the Match Operations MVP, but the wider club operating model still needs communications, finance, rewards, reporting and final polish.

| Area | Estimated completion | Current position |
|---|---:|---|
| Core Platform | 95% | Portal, routing, repositories, permissions and UI foundations are established. |
| People and Teams | 95% | People, relationships, teams and active assignments support current workflows. |
| Events and Venues | 95% | Event creation/editing, venues, audience and visibility are in place. |
| Attendance | 95% | RSVP, availability, attendance and Team Attendance Workspace are operational. |
| Matchday Hub | 90% | Preparation and dynamic readiness are integrated; reporting remains next. |
| Match Mode | 88% | Lineup, state, score, goals, Rolling Substitutions, timeline, undo and reopen are complete for the MVP. |
| Communications | 10% | Early foundations only; club and team messaging remain planned. |
| Rewards | 20% | Partial foundations; a complete rewards workflow remains planned. |
| Finance | 10% | Early planning/foundations; operational finance workflows remain planned. |
| Reporting | 10% | Match Reports, Player Ratings and wider exports remain planned. |

## Current MVP Boundary

Completed football operations include Matchday Hub, Match Details, Match Mode, the Match Lineup Builder, live score/state controls, Goal Attribution, Rolling Substitutions, the incident timeline, undo/reopen workflows and the Match Readiness Dashboard.

The full club-wide MVP is not yet complete. The next product focus is Match Reports, Player Ratings and Player of the Match, followed by wider communications, rewards, finance, reporting and release polish.
